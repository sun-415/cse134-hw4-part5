## Ingredients
- 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)  
- 1 chicken thigh, diced  
- 2 eggs  
- 1/2 cup frozen peas & carrots  
- 1 green onion, chopped  
- 2 tbsp soy sauce  
- 1 tbsp oil  

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a pan and cook chicken until browned.  
2. Push chicken aside; scramble eggs in the same pan.  
3. Add rice, peas, carrots, soy sauce, and stir-fry together.  
4. Cook 2â€“3 minutes until everything is hot.  
5. Top with green onions and serve.
