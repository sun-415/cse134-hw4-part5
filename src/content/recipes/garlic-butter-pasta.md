## Ingredients

- 200g spaghetti
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Grated Parmesan, to serve

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in salted water according to package directions.
2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a pan and gently cook garlic until fragrant.
3. Toss cooked pasta with the garlic butter, season, and serve with Parmesan.
