## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 1/2 onion, diced  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes  
- 1 cup vegetable broth  
- 1/2 cup heavy cream (or milk)  
- Fresh basil, chopped  
- Salt & pepper  

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and sauté onions 3–4 minutes until soft.  
2. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.  
3. Pour in tomatoes and vegetable broth; simmer 10 minutes.  
4. Stir in cream and basil.  
5. Blend with an immersion blender for extra smoothness.  
6. Season to taste and serve warm.
